    About Me O mnie

    I come from a small town called "Wola Książęca" near Jarocin. I started my adventure with programming and robotics in secondary school and after moving to Poznań, I graduated from the Automation and Robotics profile with a master's degree in engineering. Currently, I work at Technika Spawalnicza as a robotization specialist and programmer. My main tasks in my current job include programming robots and developing a web-based management platform. One of my main interests outside of work is music. I like playing guitar and singing (I've also played in some metal bands in the past). I collect vinyl records. I like to play board games and card games (MTG) with my friends. In my spare time I watch old s-f movies and horror movies from the 80s.
